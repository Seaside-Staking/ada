Services

Secure
Delegation is non-custodial

Your ADA never leaves your wallet when delegated, protecting you from any attacks on the pool. Seaside Staking implements modern cybersecurity practices to ensure the protection of its nodes.

Reliable
Microsoft Azure guarantee

The bulk of Seaside Staking happens on the Azure servers with 24/7 uptime and easy upgradability. Extra bare-metal relay to maximize block propagation and geo-distribution.

Independent
Run by newly-wed couple

Seaside Staking believes in keeping Cardano decentralized by encouraging single pool operators to thrive. We are commited to running a maximum of one pool forever.

PIGY Token

- Seaside Staking distributes 1 PIGY for each 1 ADA delegated per epoch. (Minimum 100 ADA)

- PIGY is a Cardano based token created for stake pools to distribute. The supply of PIGY Token is locked and limited to only 100,000,000,000 tokens. 

- PIGY is the only currency that pays for the PIGY Oracle.

- Can be traded on different Cardano DEX exchanges

- more information can be found on the official PIGY website
